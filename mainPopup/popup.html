        Response Intention

        The response intention is the purpose of the response to be generated. The list is generated by the AI based on the current E-Mail. You can select one of those intentions to provide the AI with contents for the response.

        Writing Tone

        The writing tone is the style of the response. It can be formal or informal. The AI generates the response based on the selected tone, adapting in the way it writes the response.

        Urgency

        The urgency is the level of importance of the response. The AI generates the response based on the selected urgency, adapting in the way it writes the response.

        General Settings

        Under general settings, you can set your ChatGPT API key and the name you want to use in the response. The API key is required for the generator to work.

        Templates

        The AI templates allow you to provide additional information to the AI. This can help the AI generate a more accurate response. You can add up to 10 templates. An example for this is a template for the location of an event which you may be asked for frequently.
